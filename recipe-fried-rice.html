🍳 Simple Fried Rice

Leftovers? Let's glam it up 🕺✨

🧂 Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice - 1 bowl
  • 1 Egg 🥚
  • Chopped garlic & onion
  • Any veggie/meat you like
  • Soy Sauce - 1 tbsp
  • Oil + Love

🔥 How to Make It

  1. Heat oil, fry garlic & onion till golden.
  2. Crack in the egg, scramble it like your emotions 💥
  3. Add your veggies or leftovers.
  4. Throw in rice and soy sauce. Stir-stir-stir 🔄
  5. Done! Serve hot with a smile 😊
