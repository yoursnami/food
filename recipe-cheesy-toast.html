🧀 Cheesy Toast

Fast. Crispy. Melty. Mood lifter. 💖

🧂 Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread 🍞
  • Cheese (slice or shredded)
  • Butter (optional but yummy!)
  • Oregano or chili flakes if you’re feeling extra

🔥 How to Make It

  1. Butter your bread like you're pampering it 🧈
  2. Place cheese between slices (or open-face it for max melt!)
  3. Toast in pan or oven till crispy & gooey 🫠
  4. Sprinkle flakes, cut diagonally (chef vibes)
  5. Cheese pull = satisfaction unlocked 🧀💫
