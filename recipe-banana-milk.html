🍌 Banana Milk

Korean café vibes at home 🥺💛

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1 Ripe Banana 🍌
  • 1 cup Milk (any kind)
  • 1 tsp Sugar or Honey
  • Optional: Ice cubes 🧊

🌀 How to Make It

  1. Chop banana into cute little chunks ✂️
  2. Blend with milk + sugar till smooth and creamy.
  3. Serve in a cold glass with a straw 🧋
  4. Smile. Sip. Repeat. 💛
