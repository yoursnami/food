🍝 Tomato Pasta

A warm hug in a bowl 🍅✨

🧂 Ingredients

  • Pasta (any type!) - 1 cup
  • Chopped Tomatoes - 2 pcs
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Olive Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Optional: Cheese 🧀 & Basil 🌿

📝 How to Make It

  1. Boil the pasta until it's soft and dreamy.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic in olive oil (smells sooo good 🧄✨).
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  4. Mix in the drained pasta, add salt & pepper.
  5. Top with cheese if you're feeling fancy. DONE! 💃
